# Delhi Residential Planning Notes (DDA / Unified Building Bye-Laws)

## Setback Rules
DDA Unified Building Bye-Laws require front setback of 3.0m, rear setback of
3.0m, and side setbacks of 3.0m for plots between 100-500 sqm in residential use
zones. Corner plots may have relaxed side setbacks on the shorter road frontage.

## Floor Area Ratio (FAR)
Maximum FAR for residential plots in Delhi is 3.5 under the Delhi Master Plan 2021
amendments. Plots on roads wider than 24m may be eligible for additional FAR
through the premium FAR scheme.

## Parking Standards
One equivalent car space (ECS) per 100 sqm of built-up area is required. Open
parking in the front setback is permitted for plots under 300 sqm. Basement
parking is encouraged for larger developments.

## Heritage Buffer Zones
Plots within 100m of an ASI-protected monument are subject to height restrictions
(maximum 15m) and mandatory No Objection Certificate from the Heritage Conservation
Committee.

## Rainwater Harvesting
All plots over 100 sqm in Delhi must include a rainwater harvesting pit as per
DJB (Delhi Jal Board) norms. A percolation pit with minimum 1m diameter and 2m
depth is typically sufficient for residential plots.
